Gumbo

  • Time: 45 min
  • Serves: 6-8
Ingredients Quantity
butter 2 tbsp
diced green pepper 2 cups
chopped onion 2 cups
chopped garlic 1 tbsp
salt 1 tbsp
oregano 2 tbsp
black pepper 2 tbsp
sugar 2 tbsp
dried basil 2 tbsp
dried thyme 2 tbsp
bay leaves 2
red wine vinegar 1/4 cup
smoked sausage 1-2 lbs
14oz cans dark kidney beans 6
cooked white rice for serving

Step 1: In a large saucepan, melt butter.

Step 2: Add all ingredients except beans and rice and saute until vegetables are soft.

Step 3: Add beans and cook for 30 minutes over medium heat.

Step 4: Use hand blender or potato masher to mash beans until approximately 50 percent of the beans remain intact.

Step 5: Serve over cooked white rice.

Indian Fry Bread

  • Time: 20 min
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
all-purpose flour 2 cups
baking powder 2 1/2 tsp
salt 1 tsp
warm water 1 cup
oil, for frying 3 cups

Step 1: In a large bowl, combine, the flour, baking powder and salt.

Step 2: Add the warm water and mix using a fork until a dough forms.

Step 3: Turn the dough out onto a ligthly floured surface and knead for 5 minutes, then transfer to a clean bowl and cover tightly in plastic wrap. Let dough rest for 10 minutes.

Step 4: Divide the dough into 8 equal sections by pinching off golf-ball sized balls of dough, then pat and roll out the dough balls into roughly 6-inch discs on a lightly floured surface. Keep them covered with plastic wrap while you prepare to fry them.

Step 5: Heat 3 cups of oil in a large skillet or frying pan over medium heat for about 5 minutes until the oil temperature reaches between 350 and 360 degrees F.

Step 6: Working in batches, fry each disc in the hot oil until the dough is golden brown on one side, then carefully flip with tongs and fry on the other side.

Step 7: Set on a paper towel to drain oil and stick in a warm oven to stay hot while other fry bread is cooked.

Easy Homemade Spaghetti Sauce

  • Time: 2 hours
  • Serves: 8-9cups
Ingredients Quantity
90% ground beef 2 lbs
salt 1 tsp
black pepper 1/2 tsp
white onion, chopped 2 cups (about 1 large)
green bell pepper, chopped 1 1/3 cups (about 1 medium)
8oz package white mushrooms, sliced 1
garlic cloves, minced 3
salt 1 tsp
tomato paste 3/4 cup
15 oz cans tomato sauce 3
bay leaves 2
dried basil 1 tsp
dried oregano 1 tsp
3.8 oz can black olives, sliced 1
fresh parsley, chopped 1/4 cup

Step 1: In a large pot brown ground beef (until all pink is gone); sprinnkle with 1 teaspoon of salt and 1/2 teaspoon of black pepper.

Step 2: Add the mushrooms, green bell pepper, garlic and another teaspoon of salt.

Step 3: Cook until vegetables are tender.

Step 4: Add tomato paste, tomato sauce, bay leaves, basil and oregano.

Step 5: Stir until combined; simmer 1 hour on low.

Step 6: Stir in olives and parsley.

Source: Lady Behind The Curtain

Saucy Zucchini Lasagna

  • Time: 55 minutes
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
15oz container ricotta cheese 1
fresh parsley, chopped 1/4 cup
large egg 1
Easy Homemade Spaghetti Sauce 5 cups
large sliced zucchini 3
shredded mozzarella cheese 3 cups

Step 1: Preheat oven to 375 degrees.

Step 2: Spray a 9x13-inch baking dish with non-stick cooking spray.

Step 3: In a medium bowl stir together the ricotta, parsley and egg.

Step 4: Layer(into prepared dish) 1 cup sauce, 5 slices(or more) of zucchini, 1 cup ricotta mixture and 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese.

Step 5: The second layer goes like this; 4 cups of sauce, 7 slices of zucchini, remaining ricotta and the remaining 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese.

Step 6: Bake 40 minutes or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.

Source: Lady Behind The Curtain
